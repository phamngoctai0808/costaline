About Us

Costaline Hair & Beauty Supplies a family owned and operated wholesaler to the professional hair and beauty industry. Established in 2000, we are one of Sydney’s leading and longest running supplier of hair and beauty products.

We stock and supply only the very best professional products to qualified barbers, hairdressers, beauticians, and nail technicians at realistic wholesale prices. Our team is dedicated to hand picking, testing selections of top shelf skin care, hair care, men’s grooming, accessories, equipment and furniture from all around the world.
